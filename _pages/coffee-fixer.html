Espresso Precision Counselor

How to use: Set your current dose, yield, and time. Adjust the Sensory sliders to match what you tasted. Click Log & Calibrate to see specific adjustment targets for your next shot.


Heads up: This tool is a guide, not a law. Since every machine and bean is different, use these suggestions as a starting point, not gospel. Brew at your own risk, watch out for hot water, and don't blame me if you "choke" your grinder. Happy dialing!

The 1–9 Taste Scale Guide

1–3 (Under): Salty, sour, thin, sharpness.
4–5 (Balanced): 5 is the Sweet Spot. 4 is bright/fruity.
6–9 (Over): 6 is dry. 7+ is bitter, ashy, and burnt.
